About Me

Hi! My Name is Katelynn Nguyen and I am a Vietnamese-American. I was born in Florida, but raised in Piscataway, New Jersey. I am currently a college student finishing up a degree in Communications. My interest in coding began when my brother began his career as a fullstack developer at SHI. He graduated from Rutgers with a Bachelors in Finance, but continued his education through the Rutgers Coding BootCamp. He proved to me that anything is possible and it is never too late to fulfill your dreams. I am excited to embark on this coding journey and to become a certified fullstack developer myself. I have learned so much just a few weeks into the bootcamp and cannot wait to see what I will be able to accomplish by the end of this course.
